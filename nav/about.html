About Us
Welcome to the SGPA & CGPA Calculator! This tool is designed to help students calculate their Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) and Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) effortlessly.

Features:

  • Easy and intuitive interface for entering subject details and marks.
  • Supports multiple fields of study with predefined subject lists.
  • Flexible options for calculating SGPA and converting SGPA to CGPA.
  • Dark mode for comfortable usage, especially during late-night study sessions.

How to Use:

Simply navigate to the respective pages for your university or field of study, fill in the required details, and let the calculator do the rest. Make sure to input accurate information to get precise results.
